About Us

We are passionate about bringing runners together through events that celebrate endurance, discipline, and community. Our marathon is more than just a race—it’s a platform where beginners and seasoned athletes challenge their limits, push beyond boundaries, and experience the true spirit of running. With a strong focus on safety, organization, and inclusivity, we aim to create memorable race experiences that inspire a healthier and more active lifestyle.
