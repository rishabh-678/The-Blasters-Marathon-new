Best Time to Race


Choosing the right time to race plays a crucial role in performance, comfort, and overall enjoyment of a marathon. Early morning hours are widely considered the best time to run, as temperatures are cooler, humidity is lower, and air quality is generally better.

Seasonal timing is equally important. Cooler months provide ideal weather conditions for endurance running, minimizing heat stress and fatigue. Racing during favorable seasons allows runners to focus on rhythm, breathing, and strategy rather than battling extreme weather.
